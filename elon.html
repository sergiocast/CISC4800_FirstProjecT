ELON MUSK

Elon Musk, known as the real life "Tony Stark," is a South African- born American entrepreneur and engineer. His missions are to revolutionize transportation on Earth through electric cars and to get humans to Mars.

Elon is CEO of these two Companies

SpaceX

Missions

  • Send humans to Mars

  • Establish a Mars colony by 2040, with a population of 80,000 humans

Tesla

Missions

  1. Accelerate the worlds transition to sustainable energy

  2. To use sources that renew themselves, rather than sources that can be depleted

  3. Revolutionize transportation on Earth through electric cars
